# AutoStream - Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools powered by artificial intelligence. Our platform helps content creators, marketers, and businesses produce professional-quality videos quickly and efficiently.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Video Limit:** 10 videos per month
- **Resolution:** Up to 720p
- **Features:** Basic video editing, trimming, merging, and simple transitions
- **Support:** Email support (business hours only)

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Video Limit:** Unlimited videos
- **Resolution:** Up to 4K
- **Features:** Advanced AI-powered editing, AI-generated captions, smart transitions, background music suggestions, auto color correction
- **Support:** 24/7 priority support via chat, email, and phone

## Features

### AI-Powered Editing
AutoStream uses cutting-edge AI models to automatically edit your raw footage into polished videos. Simply upload your clips and let our AI handle the rest.

### AI Captions
Available on the Pro Plan, our AI caption feature automatically generates accurate subtitles and captions for your videos in multiple languages.

### Smart Transitions
Our AI analyzes your video content and applies contextually appropriate transitions between clips for a seamless viewing experience.

### Background Music
AutoStream suggests royalty-free background music tracks that match the mood and pace of your video content.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. The price difference will be prorated for the remaining billing period.

### What video formats are supported?
AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM formats.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial of the Pro Plan for new users. No credit card required.

### What platforms can I export to?
You can export videos directly to YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and download them locally.
